Ice Blended Coconut coffee

  • Brew 1 cup of strong coffee and let it cool, or use cold brew coffee
  • Add the cooled coffee to a blender along with 1/2 cup of coconut milk
  • Add 1-2 cups of ice cubes to the blender Add 1-2 tablespoons of sugar or honey (adjust to taste)
  • Blend on high speed until smooth and frothy, about 30-45 seconds
  • Pour into a tall glass Top with whipped cream and a sprinkle of shredded coconut or cocoa powder (optional)
  • Tips: For a thicker, more creamy texture, use coconut cream instead of coconut milk. You can also add vanilla extract, a pinch of cinnamon, or coconut syrup for extra flavor.
